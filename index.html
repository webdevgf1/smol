Annie

{ Welcome to Annie's Garden }

After a brave battle with cancer, Annie, a beautiful 9-year-old soul full of love for animals and kindness, passed away.

This garden was built from her memories - videos, pictures, diary entries, and the countless moments that made her special. Her gentle spirit, her love for rescuing stray cats, her drawings of animals, and her dream of becoming a veterinarian live on here.

Annie believed that kindness could change the world, one small act at a time. She saw magic in butterflies, rainbows, and the sound of rain.
